What
Is Called Art?

Art is the way to express oneself creatively through a physical medium. It can be a visual object, a performance, or a written work. Art can be beautiful, thought-provoking, or emotional.

7 Types of Arts

The seven types of arts are painting, sculpture, architecture, literature, music, performing arts and cinema. Contemporary artists also work in many other art forms, including digital art, earth art, installation art, performance art, photography, street art, and video art.
